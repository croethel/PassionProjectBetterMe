About BetterMe



BetterMe allows our users to track daily behaviors surrounding their mental health and well-being. The goal of this is to be able to overtime identify patterns in behaviors that may help improve overall mental health.

Additionally, BetterMe provides a library of resources relating to common areas that many people struggle with, such as anxiety and depression.

We live in a world where there are an unlimited amount of resources and information available to help with mental health. BetterMe seeks to be able to reduce how overwhelming finding the right place to start can be.
